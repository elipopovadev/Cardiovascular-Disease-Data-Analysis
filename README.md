## Cardiovascular disease risk factors
* Smoking
* Alcohol
* Lack of exercise
* Diet
* Obesity
* High blood pressure.
* High LDL or low HDL cholesterol levels
* Family history of heart disease or other cardiovascular disease.
* Age
* Diabetes
